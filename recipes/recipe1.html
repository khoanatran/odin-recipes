Banana Kale Protein Shake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup baby kale
  • 1 medium/large banana
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter (or other nut-based butter of choice)
  • 1 cup Quaker oats
  • 1.5 tablespoons Orgain vegan protein (or other vegan protein of choice)
  • 1.5 cups Silk Sweetened almond milk (or other nondairy milk of choice)

Directions

  1. Soak Quaker oats and vegan protein with almond milk for 30 minutes or overnight.
  2. Add the soaked mix into a blender. Also add the banana and kale on top of the mix.
  3. Blend the mix with medium to high speed until smooth. Enjoy!
